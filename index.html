My interest in web development began back in the early 2000s when Neopets was still a very big deal. I learnt HTML and CSS so I could make colourful webpages for my virtual pets and show them off.

Today, I have expanded my knowledge to include both front end and back end languages:

  • Front end:
    • HTML & CSS
    • Bootstrap and Skeleton frameworks
    • Handlebars
    • JavaScript
  • Back end:
    • Node.js
    • Express.js
    • MySQL & Sequelize

I have built a number of web apps from the ground up, including a tech blog, an employee management system, and a website generator for team member profiles. I have also worked collaboratively on two projects: a mini game-app that pairs users with Pokémon based on their favourite music, and a free e-book exchange that allows users to share book titles and add books to their reading lists.
